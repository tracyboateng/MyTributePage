About Me

I had never been a computer Science enthusiast. However, due to the fact that one cannot control the direction in which the wind blows, I found myself in Ghana Communication Technology University. I must say that the journey has not been easy, but with just the right amount of determination, I have been able to cultivate a love for Web design.

My Web Development Journey

My journey in web development began in my second year of university. We were introduced to FreeCodeCamp, a platform that offers free lessons on Web Design and development. Coupled with instructions from our lecturer and a few tutorials from youtube, I learnt how to create simple websites. I also learnt how to host these websites. My journey may not be as impressive as other web developers, but with time, and more effort put in, I am certain that I too can make a living out of Web design. I would like to acknowledge all the people that support and encourage me as I continue on this path to becoming a professional Web Designer.
